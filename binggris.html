Bahasa Inggris

Cardboard waste is solid waste from various industrial products, whether it comes from household activities or businesses such as food, electronics, and shopping items. The management of cardboard waste can be done through recycling into new products or composting. From this cardboard waste, I will present an observation report about cardboard waste.

Cardboard waste is made from cellulose or paper materials, so it has properties that are not resistant to water and tends to be easy to shape. Cardboard waste is generally made of paper.

Cardboard also has very common shapes such as blocks or cubes with dimensions of length, width, and height that form three-dimensional shapes. Cardboard is very easy to shape, so it can be made into various handicrafts such as photo frames, hanging lamps, racks, and others.

From cardboard waste, we can utilize it to make various handicrafts, household tools, and also as storage containers or baskets. In addition, recycling cardboard waste not only helps reduce the accumulation of garbage but also supports environmental sustainability and promotes creative economic activities. By wisely reusing cardboard waste, we contribute to keeping the environment clean, conserving natural resources, and raising public awareness about the importance of recycling. This small step can bring a big impact on the preservation of our planet in the future.
